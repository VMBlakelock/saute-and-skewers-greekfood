Saute & Skewers Favourites

See a few of our popular favourites

Olives and Oil

Ingredients: Black and Green Greek Olives, Garlic, Lemon, Olive Oil

Method: Chop the garlic and mix through the olives. Pour a small amount of olive oil over, Squeeze a pinch of lemon to taste. Kali orexi!

Ouzo Portokali

Ingredients: Ouzo, Orange Juice, Water, Sugar, Mint

Method: Sugar the glass rim. Pour in 4oz Ouzo over ice add the orange juice 2oz dress with a sprig of mint! Ya mas!

Fried Feta Parcels

Ingredients: Filo Pastry, Feta, Sesame Seeds

Method: Wrap fresh Feta in sheets of Filo. Fry until golden. Pour over honey and sprinkle with sesame - serve. Nostimo!
